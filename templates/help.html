Help Page

A Google account is all you need to start using 'The Traveler Book'


  • Sign in by clicking on button on the home page
  • After signing in you'll be redirected to your dash, where you can add your journeys and view existing journeys, enable share you current location if your browser asks for it

  • The form requires you to fill the journey name, the description and upload a photograph

  • On submitting a journey it will appear on the list on the left column

  • Click on a journey to add markers on the corresponding map on the right. Clicking on a marker, a form to upload pictures pops up. The image appears in the photobooth on the right
